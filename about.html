Hey!

I'm a second-year undergraduate at Brown University (Providence, R.I.) pursuing an Sc.B. in Cognitive Neuroscience. I'm also on the pre-med track, and I constantly think about the upcoming 11-13 years of school beyond undergrad.

Although cognitive neuroscience is my primary concentration, I'm also pretty enthusiastic about organic chemistry, oceanography, and computer science. I've yet to find something that melds all of these beyond a quantitative approach to marine neurotoxicology (mostly joking), but I'm plenty happy dabbling in these fields individually.

On campus, I'm an executive for multiple student organizations – B-SIDE Magazine, Hack@Brown, MAPS, Brown Model UN's BUSUN conference, NARAL, Brown Biotech, and Brown E-Sports. My leadership roles within these orgs all revolve around graphic design / social media + marketing / creative directorship.

I conduct research at two Brown CLPS labs: the 3D Information for Perception and Action lab under P.I. Fulvio Domini and the Perception, Action, & Cognition Lab under P.I. Joo-Hyun Song (they're different, I promise!) I also work as a CHEM0350 (organic chemistry I) tutor for the university.

When not hidden somewhere in the Sciences Library, I figure skate and run a blog about nail polish. (The colors behind my home page title are from a photo I took for the blog.) There's also a 95% chance that I'm plugged in to one of my playlists – my fixations rotate around dark R&B, indie/alt rock, electronica, and indie pop. Or, I might just be listening to the same record on repeat until I get horribly sick of it. Very guilty of this one.
