about me

Hey!

I'm a second-year undergraduate at Brown University (Providence, R.I.) pursuing a Sc.B. in Cognitive Neuroscience. I'm also on the pre-med track – and fairly certain I'm sticking to it.

Although cognitive neuroscience is my primary concentration, I'm also pretty enthusiastic about organic chemistry, oceanography, and computer science. I've yet to find something that melds all of these beyond a quantitative approach to marine neurotoxicology (mostly joking), but I'm plenty happy dabbling in these fields individually.

On campus, I'm fairly active in multiple student organizations – B-SIDE Magazine, Hack@Brown, MAPS, and WBRU, a Brown-student-run-but-not-officially-affiliated-with-Brown commercial alt rock radio station. My leadership roles within these orgs all revolve around graphic design / social media + marketing / creative directoring.

I am also currently employed at the 3D Information for Perception and Action lab under P.I. Fulvio Domini at Brown's Metcalf Research Laboratory.

When not working, I figure skate and run a blog about nail polish. There's also a 75% chance that I'm plugged in to one of my playlists – my fixations rotate around indie/alt rock, electronica, and indie pop. Or, I might just be listening to the same record on repeat until I get horribly sick of it. Very guilty of this one.
