about me

Hey!

I'm a second-year undergraduate at Brown University (Providence, R.I.) pursuing an Sc.B. in Cognitive Neuroscience. I'm also on the pre-med track – and fairly certain I'm sticking to it.

Although cognitive neuroscience is my primary concentration, I'm also pretty enthusiastic about organic chemistry, oceanography, and computer science. I've yet to find something that melds all of these beyond a quantitative approach to marine neurotoxicology (mostly joking), but I'm plenty happy dabbling in these fields individually.

On campus, I'm fairly active in multiple student organizations – B-SIDE Magazine, Hack@Brown, MAPS, Brown Model UN's BUSUN conference, NARAL, Brown Biotech, and Brown E-Sports. My leadership roles within these orgs all revolve around graphic design / social media + marketing / creative directorship.

I am also currently employed at the Brown 3D Information for Perception and Action lab under P.I. Fulvio Domini, and I work as a CHEM0330 (gen chem) tutor for the university.

When not hidden somewhere in the Sciences Library, I figure skate and run a blog about nail polish. There's also a 75% chance that I'm plugged in to one of my playlists – my fixations rotate around dark R&B, indie/alt rock, electronica, and indie pop. Or, I might just be listening to the same record on repeat until I get horribly sick of it. Very guilty of this one.
